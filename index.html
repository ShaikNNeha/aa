Seared Halibut Fish Tacos with Cilantro Slaw

Simple Recipe

Here’s a quick recipe for Seared Halibut Fish Tacos with Cilantro Slaw

Recipe Details

**Ingredients:** - 2 halibut fillets - 1 tbsp olive oil - 1 tsp chili powder - 1 tsp cumin - Salt & pepper, to taste - 8 small tortillas **For Cilantro Slaw:** - 2 cups shredded cabbage - 1/4 cup chopped cilantro - 1 tbsp lime juice - 2 tbsp sour cream - 1 tsp honey - Salt & pepper, to taste **Instructions:** 1. **Prepare the Cilantro Slaw:** In a bowl, mix shredded cabbage, cilantro, lime juice, sour cream, honey, salt, and pepper. Toss well and set aside. 2. **Season the Halibut:** Pat halibut fillets dry and season with chili powder, cumin, salt, and pepper. 3. **Sear the Halibut:** Heat olive oil in a pan over medium-high heat. Sear the fish for about 3-4 minutes per side, until golden and cooked through. Flake the fish with a fork. 4. **Assemble the Tacos:** Warm tortillas. Add flaked halibut and top with cilantro slaw. 5. **Serve:** Garnish with extra cilantro and lime wedges, if desired. Enjoy! Done in under 20 minutes!.

Crispy Potato twister with mayonnaise

Yummy Recipe

Here’s a tasty recipe for Potato twister with myonnaise

Recipe Details

**Potato Twister with Mayonnaise Recipe** **Ingredients:** - 2 large potatoes - Oil for deep frying - Salt to taste - 1 tsp paprika or chili powder (optional) - Mayonnaise for dipping **Instructions:** 1. Wash and peel the potatoes. 2. Insert a skewer through the center of each potato. 3. Carefully cut in a spiral while rotating the potato around the skewer. 4. Gently stretch the spiral along the skewer to separate the slices. 5. Heat oil in a deep pan and fry the potato twisters until golden and crispy. 6. Sprinkle with salt and paprika while hot. 7. Serve hot with a side of mayonnaise for dipping. Enjoy your crispy potato twisters!

Ultimate Layered Bliss SandWich

SandWich Recipe

Here’s awesome a recipe for Ultimate Layered Bliss SandWich

Recipe Details

**Ultimate Layered Bliss Sandwich Recipe** **Ingredients:** - 6 bread slices - 1/2 cup mayonnaise - 1/4 cup ketchup - 1/2 cup grated cheese - Sliced cucumber, tomato, and lettuce - Boiled egg slices (optional) - Salt and pepper to taste **Instructions:** 1. Mix mayonnaise and ketchup to create a spread. 2. Toast the bread slices lightly if preferred. 3. Spread the sauce on each slice. 4. Layer with lettuce, cucumber, tomato, egg slices, and cheese. 5. Season with salt and pepper. 6. Stack the slices to form layers. 7. Secure with toothpicks and cut into triangles. Enjoy your flavorful layered bliss sandwich!.

Zesty & Bold Italic Chicken Wings

Chicken Wings Recipe

Here’s awesome a recipe for Ultimate italic chicken

Recipe Details

**Ultimate Layered Bliss Sandwich Recipe** **Zesty & Bold Italic Chicken Wings Recipe** **Ingredients:** - 500g chicken wings - 2 tbsp olive oil - 1/4 cup hot sauce (like Sriracha) - 2 tbsp honey - 1 tbsp soy sauce - 1 tsp garlic powder - Salt and pepper to taste **Instructions:** 1. Preheat oven to 200°C (400°F). 2. Mix hot sauce, honey, soy sauce, garlic powder, salt, and pepper in a bowl. 3. Coat chicken wings with the mixture and marinate for 30 minutes. 4. Arrange wings on a baking tray lined with foil. 5. Bake for 25-30 minutes, flipping halfway, until golden and crispy. 6. Brush with extra sauce before serving. Enjoy your bold, zesty wings!.

Choco swirl Dream Supreme

**Choco Swirl Dream Supreme Recipe**

Recipe Details

**Ingredients:** - 1 cup milk - 1/4 cup dark chocolate chips - 2 tbsp cocoa powder - 1 tbsp sugar (optional) - Whipped cream for topping - Chocolate syrup for swirl **Instructions:** 1. Heat milk in a pan, add chocolate chips, cocoa powder, and sugar. 2. Stir until smooth and creamy. 3. Pour into a mug. 4. Top with whipped cream and drizzle chocolate syrup in a swirl. Enjoy your rich, dreamy chocolate delight!
